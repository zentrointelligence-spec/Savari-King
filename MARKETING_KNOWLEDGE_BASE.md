## 5. DESTINATIONS COVERED

- **Kanniyakumari** — India's southernmost tip; sunrise where the Arabian Sea,
  Bay of Bengal & Indian Ocean meet. Vivekananda Rock Memorial, Thiruvalluvar
  Statue, Kumari Amman Temple, Gandhi Memorial, Wax Museum, Sunset Point.
- **Trivandrum & Kovalam** — Kerala's capital (2,000 years of history).
  Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Napier Museum, Kovalam & Lighthouse Beach, Poovar
  Island backwaters.
- **Kochi (Cochin)** — colonial spice-trade port. Fort Kochi, Chinese Fishing
  Nets, Mattancherry Palace, Jewish Synagogue, Spice Markets, Marine Drive.
- **Madurai** — the living temple city (2,500 years). Meenakshi Amman Temple
  (14 towering gopurams), Thirumalai Nayakkar Palace, Gandhi Memorial Museum,
  Alagar Koil, jasmine market.
- **Ooty (Udhagamandalam)** — Queen of Hill Stations, 2,240 m. Nilgiri Mountain
  Railway (UNESCO), Botanical Gardens, Doddabetta Peak, tea estates.
- **Kodaikanal** — Princess of Hill Stations. Star-shaped Kodai Lake, Pillar
  Rocks, Coakers Walk, Silver Cascade falls.
- **Munnar & Thekkady** — tea estates, spice plantations, Western Ghats
  wilderness, Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary.
- **Alleppey & Marari** — Kerala backwaters, overnight private houseboat,
  palm-fringed beaches.
- **Poovar** — island backwaters, beach resort scenery.
