## 📞 NEED HELP?

**Frequently Asked Questions:**

**Q: Do I need to fill all sections?**
A: Sections marked ✅ YES are mandatory. Sections marked ⚠️ Recommended are strongly advised for a quality tour.

**Q: How many packages should I create?**
A: Minimum 1, ideally 2-3 (Standard, Comfort, Premium) to offer customers choice.

**Q: How to calculate the price?**
A: Include all costs (accommodation, transport, guides, entrance fees, specified meals) + your margin.

**Q: Can I modify a tour after publication?**
A: Yes, tours can be modified at any time.

**Q: How to handle high season pricing?**
A: Use the "Seasonal pricing" section or create special offers.
